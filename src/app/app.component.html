Information
Khayradin, located in the Apherion system, is one of the largest known worlds suitable for human life, with a population of over 50 billion between its surface, orbital stations, and moons. Previously under Hagiographic control, the system erupted into conflict after the Uinta Station Incident, as well as the outbreak of hostilities in orbit around Free Sanjak.

It's sister world, Tilimsan, lies significantly closer to the star of Apherion, and is best known for both its harsh deserts and towering polar arcologies. Firmly under the control of the House of Sand, its largest houses have pledged their might to eliminate insurgents who threaten the prosperity of the Baronies.

With Uinta Station out of commission, and a Union Relief Fleet years away, the future of this theatre is unknown.
